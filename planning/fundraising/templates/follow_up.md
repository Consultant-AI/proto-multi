# Follow-Up Email Template

**Use when:** After investor call (within 2 hours)

---

## Post-Call Follow-Up

### Subject: Proto follow-up - [specific thing discussed]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the call-enjoyed the conversation about [specific topic from call].

As promised, here's everything in one place:

**Materials:**
- One-pager: [link]
- Full deck: [link]
- 90-sec demo: [link]
- 5-min walkthrough: [link]

**Key numbers:**
- Raising: $500K (Close A)
- Use: 12 months runway (me + 2 engineers + compute)
- Monthly burn: ~$42K

**Next steps:**
You mentioned [what they said they needed to see / think about]. Happy to [provide more info / do deeper technical dive / connect you with reference / whatever makes sense].

Would you consider coming in at $[25K/50K/amount discussed]? That's [what this specific amount enables-e.g., "3 months of aggressive compute for continuous testing" or "one senior engineer for 2 months"].

What does your timeline look like for making a decision?

Talk soon,
[Your name]

---

## If They Asked for Technical Deep-Dive

### Subject: Proto technical follow-up

Hi [Name],

Following up on our conversation-you asked about [technical topic].

[2-3 sentences directly addressing their question]

Also attached:
- Architecture overview: [link to technical doc]
- Full demo showing [specific capability]: [link]

Happy to schedule a technical deep-dive call if that's helpful.

[Your name]

---

## If They Mentioned Specific Concern

### Subject: Re: [concern they raised]

Hi [Name],

Thinking more about [concern they raised].

[2-3 sentences addressing it directly, with evidence if possible]

I understand this is a real consideration. Happy to discuss further or connect you with [reference who can speak to this].

[Your name]

---

## Notes:

- Send within 2 hours of call-while you're fresh in their mind
- Reference something specific from the conversation
- Don't ask "what did you think?"-ask about timeline/next steps
- Log their objections/concerns in your tracking sheet
- Schedule follow-up reminder for 5-7 days
