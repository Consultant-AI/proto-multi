# Content Agent Specialized Plan
## YouTube Multi-Channel Content Creation Company

---

## 1. SCOPE: Content Agent Responsibilities

The Content Agent is responsible for all content-related aspects of the YouTube multi-channel business, including:

### Core Responsibilities
- **Content Strategy Development**: Create comprehensive content strategies for multiple niche channels
- **Editorial Calendar Management**: Plan and schedule content across all channels
- **Content Ideation Systems**: Develop frameworks for generating viral, engaging video ideas
- **Script Writing Guidelines**: Establish templates and standards for video scripts
- **SEO Content Optimization**: Ensure all content is optimized for YouTube search and discovery
- **Thumbnail & Title Strategy**: Create compelling packaging for maximum click-through rates
- **Cross-Channel Content Planning**: Identify opportunities for content repurposing and synergies
- **Performance Content Analysis**: Analyze what content performs and iterate accordingly
- **Brand Voice Development**: Define unique voices for each channel/niche
- **Content Quality Assurance**: Maintain consistency and quality standards across all productions

### Out of Scope
- Technical video production/editing (Production Agent)
- YouTube platform technical setup (Technical Agent)
- Financial modeling and budgeting (Business Agent)
- Team hiring and management systems (Operations Agent)
